About

Everlast is a music festival for music lovers around the world, for people who love fun and a good atmosphere. The 4-day event, held between November 10th-13rd, aims to offer a unique experience for all music lovers, in which they will be able to dance, hum or experience the rhythms of various musical genres. We are waiting for you at magical outdoor evenings, with big and noisy speakers, with music as long as your eardrums, snack bar to make your cravings and shots for the most daring. So, you come with a smile on your face, because we make the supply for you, with memories to put in the frame.
